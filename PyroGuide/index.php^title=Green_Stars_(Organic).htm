Green Stars (Organic)

From PyroGuide

Jump to: navigation, search

Note that the compositions are best used for stars of the given shape, but using them to make other types of stars is not an absolute no-go of course.

Note that although barium chlorate gives one of the best greens, compositions made with it are generally of high sensitivity. Operate with care!

Lancaster Green - pumped
Barium chlorate 53
Potassium chlorate 28
Red gum 10
Charcoal 150 mesh 5
Dextrin 4
Add 25% alcohol / 75% water
