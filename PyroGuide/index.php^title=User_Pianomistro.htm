Pianomistro
I live in eastern Maryland, on a large farm. +200 acres gives me a lot of room for pyro. I'll be doing a lot of testing and reporting on some compositions, and probably writing quite a few tutorials in the near future.




Things I'm currently working on:
Willow Shell Tutorial, Rolled Stars, Dragon Eggs, the list goes on...

Things up and coming in the near future:
Revisions on my Star Tooling page, Making a Ball Mill, Making a Star Roller, Electronic Firing Boxes (how to wire and make them yourself)

Pianomistro's Custom Tools As many of you know, I'm into making custom pyro tooling, including star plates, crossette pumps, and rocket tooling sets. If anyone is interested, PM me on the forum, or email me at pianomistro@hotmail.com. My website for my tooling is http://pianomistroscustomtools.110mb.com/.
